Core Philosophy & Classroom Values

Educational Philosophy

I believe in a child-centered, inclusive classroom where students are not passive listeners but active creators. Pedagogy should be dynamic, linking historical contexts and geographical perspectives with real-world problems.

By leveraging technology—such as interactive smart boards and online classroom systems—I bridge the gap between traditional learning and modern, hybrid educational needs, fostering critical thinking and collaborative problem-solving.

"Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think."
